## Lesson 20. **be**, **bei**, **be'o** (sumti links into selbri)

<pixra url="/assets/pixra/books/first-lojban/icons/koc1.png" caption="Koshon" definition=""></pixra>

We widen selbri → practically **sumti** again (like lesson 8).

<speaker sprite="sor3">Déjà vu!</speaker>
<pixra url="/assets/pixra/books/first-lojban/icons/koc4.png" caption="Koshon" definition=""></pixra>

Same pattern: **be** / **bei** **fill places** of the attached brivla inside a **sumti description**.

> mi citka lo plise
> _I eat an apple. Predicate **citka** has x1 eater, x2 food._


To mean “apple-eater” as a property: **citka be lo plise** — x1 eats apples.

<pixra url="/assets/pixra/books/first-lojban/icons/sor7.png" caption="Sora" definition=""></pixra>

Then **lo citka be lo plise** = “apple-eater(s).”

<dl class="drani">
<dt>be</dt>
<dd>connects first sumti into x2 of preceding brivla</dd>
<dt>bei</dt>
<dd>further sumti (x3, x4, …)</dd>
<dt>be'o</dt>
<dd>closes be-chain when needed</dd>
</dl>


> mi klama lo klaji **be** la .latcmatcad.
> _I go to the street(s) of Latcmatcad._

> lo ckule **be** fi lo lojbo **bei** fu la .mikan. cu se stuzi ma
> _The Lojban school run by Mikan is located where?_

> ko na pencu lo nenri **be** lo tanxe **be** fi lo pelji
> _Don’t touch what’s inside the paper box._


<pixra url="/assets/pixra/books/first-lojban/icons/sor1.png" caption="Sora" definition=""></pixra>

Why **bei** after the first sumti?

<pixra url="/assets/pixra/books/first-lojban/icons/koc1.png" caption="Koshon" definition=""></pixra>

If every link were **be**, nested structures would parse ambiguously — **bei** keeps grouping unique.

<pixra url="/assets/pixra/books/first-lojban/icons/koc5.png" caption="Koshon" definition=""></pixra>

**be**-phrases work inside **tanru**; sometimes **be'o** is required to stop the chain.

**Paraphrase toolkit:** **ti lumci be lo dinju be'o minji** vs **ti dinju lumci minji** vs **ti me lo minji poi ke'a lumci lo dinju** vs **ti minji lo nu lumci lo dinju**.
