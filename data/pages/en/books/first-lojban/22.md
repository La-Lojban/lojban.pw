## Lesson 22. Quotation

<pixra url="/assets/pixra/books/first-lojban/icons/koc1.png" caption="Koshon" definition=""></pixra>

**Direct** vs **indirect** quotation; plus handy **anaphora** and **la'e**.

### Grammatical Lojban — **lu … li'u**

<dl class="drani">
<dt>lu</dt>
<dd>open grammatical Lojban quote</dd>
<dt>li'u</dt>
<dd>close (<strong>li'u</strong>, not lu'u)</dd>
</dl>


> do cusku **lu mi prami do li'u** mi
> _You said to me, “mi prami do.”_


### Ungrammatical / raw Lojban text — **lo'u … le'u**

### One Lojban word — **zo**

> mi se cmene **zo .soran.**
> _My name is the word “soran.”_

> **zo pu** cu cmavo
> _**pu** is a cmavo._


**zo** quotes **one** word — **zo puzi** still only quotes **pu**, not **puzi**.

### Non-Lojban — **zoi X … X**

**X** is a delimiter word (often **zoi zoi … zoi** “zoi zoi blah zoi”).

### Indirect speech — **du'u** / **se du'u**

> do cusku **lo se du'u** mi prami do **kei** mi
> _You said (something expressible as) that I love you._


### Discourse sumti — **di'u**, **di'e**, …

Often **di'u** “the last utterance”, **di'e** “the next utterance.”

### **la'e** — “the referent of”

> .e'o mi'o zgana **la'e zoi py. My Neighbor Totoro py.**

> le ninmu cu cisma .i mi gleki **la'e di'u**
> _She smiled; I’m happy about that (what was said)._


### **la'o** — non-Lojban names with delimiters

> .e'o mi'o zgana **la'o py.** My Neighbor Totoro **py.**
