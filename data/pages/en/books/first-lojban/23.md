**Vowel** picks **meaning**; **consonant shape** picks **what is connected**.

**na** / **nai** on connectives give negated flavours (NAND, NOR, …) — learn the common ones from tables in **CLL** / cheat-sheets.

### Sentence connectives **.i jV**

> ti glare **.ije** ta lenku
> _This is hot **and** that is cold._

> mi citka **.ija** mi sipna
> _I eat **or** I sleep._

> do citka **.ije'i** do sipna ― .ijenai
> _Eat or sleep? — Eat, not sleep._


### Sumti connectives **.V**

> mi cortu lo cutne **.e** lo stedu
> _My chest **and** head hurt._


### **gi'V** — bridi-tail connectives (shared front)

> mi klama lo zarci **gi'e** te vecnu lo tsasmani
> _I go to the shop **and** buy a gorilla._
