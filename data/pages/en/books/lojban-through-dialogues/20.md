**New words**

| Lojban | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| taske | x₁ is thirsty/tired — here used as "tired, weary" |
| kufra | x₁ feels comfortable / at ease |
| zabna | nice, pleasant, agreeable (of situations, days, places) |
| pluka | x₁ is pleasant to x₂ |
| cinmo | x₁ feels emotion x₂ about x₃ — **mi cinmo gleki** = "I feel happy" |

> **Grammar note:**
> **zabna** describes situations and conditions: **le bavlamdei cu zabna** = "tomorrow is nice." **pluka** describes what is pleasant *to someone*: **lo nu tavla do kei cu pluka mi** = "the event of talking to you is pleasant to me." **lo nu … kei** (from Lesson 18) turns any predicate into an event-noun; **kei** closes the clause. **cinmo** is the predicate for feeling: **mi cinmo gleki** = "I feel a happiness-type emotion."
