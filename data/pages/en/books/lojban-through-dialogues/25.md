## Lesson 25. Paying Bills

<speaker sprite="lif2">Mentu asks about living costs. Monthly bills, companies, and the useful word **ro** — "every."</speaker>

| Lojban | English |
|--------|---------|
| xu lo dikca ca kargu | Is electricity expensive now? |
| mi na djuno .i mi na pleji | I don't know. I don't pay. |
| xu do pleji le kagni | Do you pay the company? |
| ca ro detma'i mi pleji | Every calendar month I pay. |
| mi pleji lo meryru'u be li re no | I pay 20 US dollars. |
| le dikca cu tolkargu | Electricity is cheap. |

**New words**

| Lojban | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| pleji | x₁ pays x₂ to x₃ for x₄ |
| kagni | company, organization, firm |
| dikca | electricity |
| detma'i | calendar month |
| ro | every / all |

> **Grammar note:** **ro** means "every." **ca ro detma'i** = "every calendar month." It can quantify any noun: **ro zdani** = "every home," **ro panzi** = "every child."

> **Say in Lojban:** Every month I pay. *(ca ro detma'i mi pleji)*
