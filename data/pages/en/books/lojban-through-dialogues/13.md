## Lesson 13. At the Restaurant

<speaker sprite="lif2">Lifri takes Mentu and Linto to a restaurant. Time to learn food words — all within structures you already know.</speaker>

| Lojban | English |
|--------|---------|
| .ai mi klama lo gusta | I'm going to go to a restaurant. |
| .au mi citka lo cidja | I want to eat some food. |
| .au mi pinxe lo jisra | I want to drink some juice. |
| .au mi citka lo titnanba | I want to eat some cookies. |
| xu do .au citka lo cidja | Do you want to eat food? |
| go'i | Yes. |
| .ai mi citka lo stasu | I'm going to eat soup. |

**New words**

| Lojban | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| gusta | restaurant |
| cidja | food |
| jisra | juice |
| titnanba | cookies / sweet baked goods |

> **Grammar note:** No new grammar this lesson — practise the structures you know (**lo** for general things, **.au** for desire, **.ai** for intent) with fresh vocabulary. Notice how **lo gusta** = "a restaurant," **lo cidja** = "some food," **lo jisra** = "some juice."

### Anticipation prompts

> **Say in Lojban:** I'm going to a restaurant. *(.ai mi klama lo gusta)*

> **Say in Lojban:** I want to eat food. *(.au mi citka lo cidja)*

> **Say in Lojban:** I want juice. *(.au mi pinxe lo jisra)*

> **Say in Lojban:** I want cookies. *(.au mi citka lo titnanba)*
