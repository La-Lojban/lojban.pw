## Lesson 4. I Talk to You

<speaker sprite="lif2">Lifri shows Mentu how to say things about himself and others — the first full sentences with a subject and a predicate.</speaker>

| Lojban | English |
|--------|---------|
| mi tavla do | I talk to you. |
| do tavla mi | You talk to me. |
| mi'o tavla | We talk (you and I). |
| .e'u mi'o tavla | Let's talk. |
| do tavla mi | You talk to me. |
| mi tavla do | I talk to you. |

**New words**

| Lojban | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| mi | I / me |
| tavla | x₁ talks to x₂ about x₃ in language x₄ |
| mi'o | we — you and I together |
| .e'u | let's … / I suggest … (attitudinal: suggestion) |

> **Grammar note:** Every Lojban predicate has numbered slots. The first slot (x₁) is the actor; the second (x₂) is the target. Order: **x₁ → predicate → x₂ → …** So **mi tavla do** = "I (x₁) talk-to you (x₂)." Swap them and the meaning reverses: **do tavla mi** = "you talk to me."
