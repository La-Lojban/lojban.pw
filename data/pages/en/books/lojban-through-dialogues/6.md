## Lesson 6. Where Are You?

<speaker sprite="lif2">Lifri, Mentu, and Linto are making plans. They need to say where things are and where they're going.</speaker>

| Lojban | English |
|--------|---------|
| mi zvati le zdani | I'm at home. |
| do zvati ma | Where are you? |
| mi zvati le panka | I'm at the park. |
| mi klama le panka | I go to the park. |
| do klama ma | Where do you go? |
| mi klama le zdani | I go home. |

**New words**

| Lojban | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| zvati | x₁ is present at / located at x₂ |
| le | the — marks a specific thing the speaker has in mind |
| zdani | home, dwelling |
| klama | x₁ goes to x₂ (from x₃ via x₄ by means x₅) |
| panka | park |

> **Grammar note:** **le** is like the English "the" — it marks something specific that the speaker has in mind. **le panka** = "the park (I'm thinking of)." For now, treat it as "the."
