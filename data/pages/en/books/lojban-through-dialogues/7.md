**New words**

| Lojban | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| cerni | morning |
| vanci | evening |
| nicte | night |
| donri | daytime / daylight period — **donri coi** = "good day!" |
| ca | at (time) — "now" or "at the time of" |
| gleki | x₁ is happy about x₂ |

> **Grammar note:**
> **ca le cerni** means "at the morning / in the morning." **ca** places the sentence at a particular time. It stacks naturally: **ca le vanci mi klama** = "in the evening I go." **donri coi** pairs **donri** (daytime) with **coi** exactly like **cerni coi** — a natural daytime greeting.
