|--------|---------|

| no | zero (0) |

> **Grammar note:**
> Digits chain left to right: **pano** = "1-0" = 10, **pare** = "1-2" = 12, **reno** = "2-0" = 20. The remaining digits **xa** (6), **ze** (7), **bi** (8), **so** (9), **no** (0) complete the set — practise them, but the first five are enough for now.
