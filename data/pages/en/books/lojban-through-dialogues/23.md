## Lesson 23. Discussing Plans

<speaker sprite="lif2">Lifri and Mentu discuss what to do next. Two useful words: **casnu** (to discuss) and **prami** (to love). And **lo nu** continues to build complex sentences.</speaker>

| Lojban | English |
|--------|---------|
| pei mi'o casnu | How about we discuss something? |
| tu'a le bavlamdei cu zabna | As for tomorrow — that works. |
| mi prami lo nu tadni la .lojban. | I love studying Lojban. |
| mi prami do | I love you. |
| .e'u mi'o casnu ca le vanci | Let's discuss in the evening. |
| je'e | Got it. |

**New words**

| Lojban | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| casnu | x₁ discusses x₂ |
| zabna | nice, pleasant, agreeable |
| prami | x₁ loves x₂ |
| tadni | x₁ studies x₂ |

> **Grammar note:** **lo nu** continues to turn predicates into event-nouns. **mi prami lo nu tadni la .lojban.** = "I love [the event of] studying Lojban." You can nest any predicate inside **lo nu**: **lo nu mi sipna**, **lo nu do tavla**, etc.
